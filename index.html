What AIVI measures

AIVI converts “AI understanding” into a single index, backed by four layers you can improve.

Structure & Crawlability

Whether AI agents can access, parse, and traverse your site without friction.

Authority & Trust

Signals that help AI treat you as real and reliable: identity, provenance, consistency.

Clarity & Language

How clearly AI can summarize what you do — without guessing or hallucinating.

Content–AI Alignment

How well your content answers intent with reusable structure (entities, coverage, support).

Each layer contributes to your AI Visibility Index reading — a single, interpretable signal of how AI systems perceive your presence. Designed to be explainable enough to act on, without turning your site into a robot brochure.
