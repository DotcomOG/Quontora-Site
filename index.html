What AIVI measures

AIVI turns the messy reality of “AI understanding” into a single index — and four clear layers you can improve.

Structure & Crawlability

Can AI agents access, parse, and traverse your site cleanly — or do they hit friction, dead ends, and ambiguity?

Authority & Trust

Signals that make AI more willing to treat you as real and reliable: identity clarity, provenance, and consistency.

Clarity & Language

Do you communicate what you do in a way AI can compress into a confident summary — without guessing?

Content–AI Alignment

How well your content answers real intent with reusable structure: definitions, entities, coverage, and internal support.

Each layer contributes to your AI Visibility Index reading — a single, interpretable signal of how AI systems perceive your presence. It’s designed to be explainable enough to act on, without turning your site into a robot brochure.
